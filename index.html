BRIEF OVERVIEW

The Gaborone Community Orchestra (GCO) was found in 2019, by Mr. Darius Kamwi who is also the conductor and director of this amaizing group of talented musicians.

Directed by Darius Kamwi

The motive behind the GCO was to bring musicians from different countries, races or origin into a community of music. This GCO now stands as the first and one of the biggest ochestra in Botswana and has emerged eversince performing in various concerts (15+), featuring in BTV; Tsholofelo Foundation christmas carols, and many more events; supporting the nation and culture of Botswana.

The One and Only Standard Orchestra in Botswana

A distinguished ensemble of more than 30 talented musicians, the orchestra brings together a vibrant community of talent and cultural diversity.

A Truly Global Collective

Our members represent a wide international spectrum, originating from: Botswana, Ireland, Germany, Russia, the United Kingdom, the United States, India, South Africa, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

A Multigenerational Ensemble

With ages ranging from 8 to over 50, the orchestra blends youthful energy with seasoned expertise, shaping a rich and dynamic musical experience.

A Community of Professionals and Creatives

Our membership includes music teachers, artists, students, and dedicated performers, all united by a shared commitment to excellence.
